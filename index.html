About us

Pawriwar is an NGO based in South Delhi who feed, foster, rescue and treat stray animals.

The unfortunate circumstances of COVID-19 combined with the re-development of the Sarojini Nagar has brought hundreds of dogs on the streets, abandoned.

Pawriwar took up the initiative to feed, rescue and treat these homeless stray dogs, everyday.

Be the voice for the
voiceless

By doing the work we do at Pawriwar, we support the animals who are in dire need of human help. We try our best to tackle their challenges. We give them food to eat, take care of their injuries, try to keep them in foster care for healing, and help them find a new home.

We would appeal all of you to try to do your best in making an animal's life better, in any way possible.
