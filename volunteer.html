Are you willing to join the fight to protect animals?
We are always looking for good people to join us in this selfless journey. If you are willing to help us out with medical, operational, fundraising activities and find your peace in working with animals, you can take the initiative in your area!
Your efforts can bring a smile on someone's face, or
save someone's life.
What can volunteers do?
The volunteering programme is integrated with daily feeding drives.We operate a structured programme to feed and help stray animals.
You will primarily socialize with puppies, cats and dogs. Almost all of our animals are ex-street dogs and cats who have never been indoors and need to learn to feel comfortable around people.
You can also Coordinate resuces for animals in distress and spreading awareness on social media.
Join us and make life better for animals
You may lose your mind in the process,
but you will surely find your soul.
