How can you help us?

Sponsor A Meal
Give an animal a meal that they deserve. By this donation, you will be paying for the meal of an animal (cat or a dog) for a day. To know more about the meals, click here.
Sponsor A month's meal
Treat an animal with meals that you’d enjoy. By this donation, you will be paying for the meal of an animal (cat or a dog) for a month. To know more about the meals, click here.
Sponsor Medical care for a month
Sponsor the rescue-care, foster-care and injuries and wounds treatment of animals for a month, to know more about our operations, click here.
Donate whatever you can
Please try to donate regularly or sponsor Pawriwar with recurring payments. This helps in giving us a steady funding to support our operations.
