Our Operations


Pawriwar is an NGO based in South Delhi, specifically the Sarojini
Nagar redevelopmental area, where due to the destruction of existing
housing complexes and relocation of the people living there, most of
the dogs are just left alone which were previously fed and pet by the
community of people living there.

We at Pawriwar are a small team of animal-lovers who feeds the dogs
and cats of this area everyday, without fail. We also rescue the dogs
who are either injured or in a bad health situation and we try to foster
them till they get back up on their feet, fit and healthy.

The Food


The food that we feed to the animals as a meal is prepared by
partnering with a local restaurant. There is a feeding which takes
place every single day in the demolished areas of Sarojini Nagar. In the
meals, for dogs we have a special mixture of Chicken and Rice, the
chicken comes from the restaurant’s chicken waste, also known as
“Chaatan”. This method also ensures no extra killings of chickens. The
food does not consist of any Masalas except Haldi.
And most importantly, Dogs LOVE chicken rice and they enjoy eating it
which ensures no wastage of food and no Food related litter on the
ground. The food is served on Newspaper. The food for cats is simply
bought from the stores as there isn’t a huge turnout of cats in the area
while feeding, plus they are also driven away by the bad dogs in the area.
